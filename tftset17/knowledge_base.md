## 1. SET MECHANIC — REALM OF THE GODS

Set 17's set mechanic is a **celestial marketplace** that replaces TFT's traditional carousel.

- At the start of every game, **2 gods are randomly selected** and travel to the Realm.
- In the middle of **Stages 2, 3, and 4**, players travel back to the Realm to choose between offerings from each of the two gods.
- After selecting a god offering, **Pengu** provides gifts based on your current lobby placement.
- **Align with a god** by choosing their offerings at least **2 times** across these stages.
- On **Stage 4-7**, your favored god blesses you with a **special boon** unique to them.
- For the rest of the game, your god returns each stage to offer more rewards.
